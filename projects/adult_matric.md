# Adult Matric Registration

In South Africa, the **Adult Matric** is officially known as the **Senior Certificate (Amended)**. It is a government-recognized qualification for adult learners who wish to complete their Grade 12.

## General Requirements

To register for the Senior Certificate (Amended), you must meet the following criteria:

*   **Age Requirement:** You must be **21 years or older** at the time you write the final examinations.
*   **Prior Education:** You must have **at least ONE** of the following:
    *   **EITHER** a **Grade 9** school report (or Standard 7) stating you have passed.
    *   **OR** a General Education and Training Certificate (GETC) for Adult Basic Education and Training (**ABET Level 4**).
    *   **OR** an incomplete Senior Certificate / National Senior Certificate (NSC).

*(If you do not have Grade 9 or ABET Level 4, please see the **Bridging Requirements** page.)*

## How It Works

*   **100% Exams:** Unlike the school-based matric, the Adult Matric is based **100% on final examinations**. There are no assignments or internal school-based assessments throughout the year.
*   **Subjects:** You must write **six subjects**, which must include:
    *   Two official languages (one Home Language, one First Additional Language).
    *   Mathematics or Mathematical Literacy.
    *   Three elective subjects.
*   **Passing Requirements:** You generally need to pass at least five of your six subjects to obtain the certificate (typically passing three subjects at 40% and two subjects at 30%).

## How to Register

Registration typically opens annually (often around October for the following year's May/June exams). 

1.  **Online Portal:** You can register through the Department of Basic Education (DBE) online portal at **[www.eservices.gov.za](https://www.eservices.gov.za)**.
2.  **In-Person:** You can register at any provincial education district office.

You can study on your own using textbooks, or you can enroll with private distance learning colleges or adult learning centers to get study materials and tutor support.
