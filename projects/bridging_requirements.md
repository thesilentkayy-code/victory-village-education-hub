# Bridging Requirements (If You Do Not Have Grade 9)

In South Africa, you generally need to have completed **Grade 9** (or hold an equivalent qualification) as a prerequisite to register for the Adult Matric. Because you do not have a Grade 9 certificate, you cannot register for the Adult Matric directly. 

However, there is a clear and official pathway to help you reach that goal!

## Your First Step: ABET Level 4

Since you do not have a Grade 9 certificate, your immediate step is to obtain a qualification that is recognized as its equivalent.

*   **What you need:** You need to complete **ABET Level 4** (Adult Basic Education and Training). 
*   **Why it matters:** ABET Level 4 is registered at NQF Level 1 and is officially recognized as the equivalent of a Grade 9 pass.
*   **Where to go:** You can enroll in ABET classes at public adult learning centers. It is highly recommended to contact your nearest **Department of Basic Education (DBE) district office** to find the most suitable and affordable center in your area.
*   **What if I struggle with reading and writing?** There are no strict entry requirements for lower levels (ABET levels 1–3). You can start at the beginning to build foundational skills in literacy and numeracy needed to succeed at ABET Level 4.

## Next Steps

Once you have successfully completed your ABET Level 4 (GETC) qualification, you will meet the entry requirement for the Adult Matric (Amended Senior Certificate).

**Important Note:** Avoid paying large sums of money to institutions claiming they can "bypass" these requirements. The official route requires you to hold a Grade 9 (or equivalent) qualification before you can tackle the matric curriculum. Your best and most affordable option is through public adult learning centers.
