# Welcome to the Victory Village Education Hub

*"If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together."*

Welcome to the Victory Village Education index. This resource is designed specifically for residents who are looking to advance their education, earn their matric equivalent, or find out what bridging options are available. 

Whether you left school before Grade 9 or you are looking to finish your matric as an adult, there is a path forward. Education opens doors to new employment opportunities, personal growth, and self-sufficiency.

## How to Use This Hub

Please use the navigation menu on the left to explore the topics relevant to your situation:

*   **Bridging (No Grade 9)**: If you do not have a Grade 9 certificate, you must start here to learn about ABET Level 4.
*   **Adult Matric Registration**: If you already have your Grade 9 or equivalent, learn how to register for the Amended Senior Certificate.
*   **The GED: Pros & Cons**: Learn about this international alternative to the South African Matric, and see if it's right for you.

## Support & Guidance

Remember that you are not alone on this journey. The staff and your fellow residents at Victory Village are here to support you. You can take things one step at a time!

## Summary of Estimated Costs
Planning your educational journey also means budgeting for the costs involved. Here is a rough guide:

*   **Required Documentation (ID & School Report):**
    *   To register for any of these options, you *must* have a valid South African ID and your most recent school report. 
    *   If you have lost your ID, a replacement from Home Affairs costs **R140**. If you need your school report, you will have to apply at your previous school or the Department of Education, which may also charge a small administration fee.
*   **Adult Matric (Amended Senior Certificate):**
    *   **Exams:** Registration and writing the exams with the Department of Basic Education is **100% Free**.
    *   **Study Materials:** Free if you use government "Mind the Gap" guides. Commercial study guides cost around R200 - R300 each. Private college tuition (optional) can cost thousands of Rands.
*   **Bridging Course (ABET Level 4):**
    *   Public adult learning centers are highly subsidized and are often **Free or very low cost**.
*   **The GED:**
    *   **Exams:** Approximately **$80 USD (± R1,500)** per subject. You need to write 4 subjects, bringing the total exam cost to roughly **R6,000**.
    *   **Preparation:** Optional online prep courses usually cost around R3,000 per year.
