# Adult Matric Subject Choices

When registering for your Adult Matric (officially the **Amended Senior Certificate** or ASC), you must select a total of **six subjects**. 

Unlike the high school National Senior Certificate (NSC), **Life Orientation is NOT a required subject** for adult learners.

## Compulsory Subjects
You must choose **three** compulsory subjects based on the following rules:

1.  **First Official Language:** Must be taken at the **Home Language** level.
2.  **Second Official Language:** Must be taken at the **First Additional Language** level (or Home Language).
3.  **Mathematics Choice:** You must choose either **Mathematics** OR **Mathematical Literacy**.

## Elective Subjects
You must choose **three** elective subjects. You should choose these based on your interests, previous schooling, and future career or university goals. Popular options include:

*   Accounting
*   Business Studies
*   Economics
*   Geography
*   History
*   Life Sciences (Biology)
*   Physical Sciences (Physics & Chemistry)
*   Computer Applications Technology (CAT)
*   Tourism
*   Agricultural Sciences

## Important Tips for Choosing Subjects

*   **University Entrance (Bachelor's Pass):** If your goal is to study for a degree at a university, you must check the admission requirements for the specific degree you want. For example, Nursing, Engineering, or Science degrees usually require pure Mathematics and Physical Sciences or Life Sciences.
*   **Keep It Realistic:** If you struggled with pure Mathematics in the past and you want to study Humanities, Law, or Business, taking **Mathematical Literacy** is a safer option that can still get you a Bachelor's pass.
*   **Language Choices:** Pick the languages you are most comfortable speaking and writing.
